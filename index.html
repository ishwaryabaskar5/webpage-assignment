The love for coffee was the reason it all begun in 1999, in Patras. 2006 was a milestone as we decided to set up our own coffee processing unit by investing in equipment and know-how. In 2009, with the empowerment of the team, we decided to set the goals very high and envisioned something completely fresh, innovative and original; creating a new experience for quality coffee-on-the-go.

Today, the public's response is translated into numbers. It is enough to think that our network lists more than 400 stores in Greece and abroad.

By the end of 2016, the Coffee Island family grew even more, going even abroad. With Knowledge, Engagement and Love, one more coffee grinder was added to our team in London in the UK's most competitive market! In May 2017, the company expanded to the Canadian market by opening its first store in Toronto.
